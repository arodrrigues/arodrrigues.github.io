Uploading Images to the School Gallery

To upload your artwork to the school gallery allows for anyone to see your artwork on the gallery page. This will also allow your artwork to be viewed in your personal gallery. To do so, click the Gallery link at the top left of your screen in the nav bar. Once you have reached the gallery page, click the Add Art button at the top left of your screen in the nav bar. Once you have done so, your art will appear in the school gallery as well as your personal gallery.





Uplaoding Images to Personal Gallery

To upload your artwork to your own personal gallery ensures that only you will be able to view this artwork. This means that your artwork will not be included in the school gallery as well. To do so, click the My Art link at the top left of your screen in the nav bar. Once you have reached the gallery page, click the Add Art button at the top left of your screen in the nav bar. Once you have done so, your art will appear in your personal gallery (My Art page) and not the school gallery.
