Camp

For the past 6 summers I have been spending the month of July at Camp. Camp to me is a place where I feel free and safe to fully show who I am and try out new things. This summer I have accepeted a counsellor position at Onondaga camp and am excited to give younger campers the full camp experience.

Mountains

Out of all the vacations my family and I have been on, mountainous places remain my favorite. Whether it be going to whistler to ski over chrismats or going to alberta during the summer to spend time on a ranch and ride horses, mountainous places have always been my favorite.

Sport

Sports have always been my favorite activity during my free time with skiing being my favourite. The other sports I enjoy playing are basketball, soccer and football. Skiing is my favorite as I get to travel to the most places and compete against the top atheltes in North America.

What I do..

I am currently a student in year 11 at Upper Canada College. I am currently taking HL Language and Literature, SL Computer Science, SL Latin, SL Math Analysis and Approaches, HL World History, HL Global Politics, and Theory of Knowledge. The sports I participate in competeivley are Alpine Skiing (U16 Ontario Provincial Team), and football (JV UCC Team).

During the summer of 2020 I spent my time working as a junior landscaper in Collingwood Ontario. During the fall and spring seasons I work as a Flag Football Coach and Referee at Mooredale and during the winter work as a skating instructor and hockey referee.
